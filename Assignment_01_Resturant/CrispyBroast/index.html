Crispy Broast

Crispy broast, also known as broasted chicken, is a mouthwatering and flavorful dish that has gained popularity worldwide. It is a unique cooking technique that combines the best of frying and pressure cooking to create a perfectly crispy and tender chicken.

To prepare crispy broast, chicken pieces are marinated in a seasoned mixture of spices and herbs, allowing the flavors to penetrate the meat. The marinated chicken is then coated in a special batter or breading mixture, which may include a combination of flour, breadcrumbs, and spices.

The chicken is cooked in a broaster machine, which uses a combination of pressure and hot oil to ensure even cooking and exceptional crispiness. The result is golden-brown chicken with a crunchy exterior and juicy, flavorful meat.

Crispy broast is often served with a side of fries, coleslaw, or a fresh salad. It is a popular choice for quick meals, family gatherings, or even as a special treat. The combination of crispy texture and flavorful chicken makes it a satisfying and delightful dish for chicken lovers everywhere.
