Seekh Kebab

Club Seekh Kebab is a tantalizing fusion dish that combines the best of two culinary worlds: the classic club sandwich and the traditional seekh kebab. This delectable creation features succulent and flavorful skewered kebabs nestled between layers of toasted bread, fresh vegetables, and zesty sauces.

The star of the dish is the seekh kebab, which is made by marinating minced meat (usually lamb or beef) with a blend of aromatic spices, such as cumin, coriander, ginger, and garlic. The meat is then molded onto skewers and grilled to perfection, resulting in smoky and juicy kebabs that are packed with robust flavors.

To assemble the Club Seekh Kebab, the grilled kebabs are gently placed between slices of toasted bread, accompanied by crisp lettuce, sliced tomatoes, onions, and sometimes cheese. Creamy sauces like mint yogurt or tangy chutneys are generously drizzled over the kebabs, adding an extra layer of indulgence.

The combination of tender kebabs, toasted bread, fresh vegetables, and flavorful sauces creates a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. The Club Seekh Kebab offers a satisfying and fulfilling meal that is perfect for both casual lunches and hearty dinners. It's a unique and delightful twist on two beloved dishes that is sure to please any food enthusiast.
