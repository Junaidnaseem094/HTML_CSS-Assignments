Dhaga Kebab

Dhaga kebab, also known as seekh kebab or skewered kebab, is a popular Indian and Pakistani delicacy that is loved for its succulent and smoky flavors. This kebab variation involves marinating minced or ground meat, typically chicken, lamb, or beef, with a blend of aromatic spices and herbs.

The marinated meat is then threaded onto skewers, which gives the kebab its distinct shape. The skewers are traditionally made of metal or bamboo and are cooked over an open flame or on a grill, allowing the meat to acquire a delightful charred and smoky flavor.

Dhaga kebabs are often seasoned with spices like cumin, coriander, paprika, and garam masala, along with ingredients like ginger, garlic, and fresh herbs such as cilantro or mint. The kebabs are grilled to perfection, ensuring a juicy and tender texture.

These flavorful kebabs are commonly served with naan bread, mint chutney, and a side of salad or raita (yogurt-based dip). Dhaga kebabs are a popular choice for barbecues, street food stalls, or as appetizers at festive gatherings, offering a delightful blend of smoky, aromatic, and savory flavors that are sure to please your taste buds.
